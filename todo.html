Things to Do

Maui offers countless adventures and experiences. Here are some of our favorite recommendations:

Beach Activities

  • Snorkel at Molokini Crater
  • Learn to surf at Kaanapali Beach
  • Watch sunset at Makena Beach
  • Take a catamaran cruise

Nature & Adventure

  • Drive the Road to Hana
  • Watch sunrise at Haleakala National Park
  • Hike the Pipiwai Trail
  • Visit the Iao Valley State Park

Food & Culture

  • Attend a traditional luau
  • Visit the Maui Ocean Center
  • Shop at Lahaina's Front Street
  • Try local food at Paia Fish Market
